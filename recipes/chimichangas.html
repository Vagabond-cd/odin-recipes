Chimichangas with Sourcream Sauce

Chickens chimichangas

I love chimichangas. And I guarantee you will too after following this recipe. For the uninformed a chimichanga is essentially a deep-fried burrito. Typically comprised of some kind of shredded or ground meat(this recipes uses chicken but you can substitute for just about anything.),cheese, onions and peppers, all wrapped up in a flour tortilla and deep-fried till golden brown. I personally enjoy them with a fresh salad of lettuce, onions, and guacamole. A healhy drizzle of sourcream sauce makes it even better.

Ingredients

-Chicken Chimichangas-

  • 1 lbs(~450g) skinless boneless chicken breasts or thighs
  • 3 cups(~700ml) of water
  • 1 Tbsp(~14g) chili powder
  • 1 tsp(~5.5g) salt
  • 1 tsp(~5.5g) ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp(~3g) ground black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp(~3g) garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp(~3g) onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp(1.5g) cayenne pepper
  • 1(7oz)(~200g)can of green chiles (If you want more heat substitute with fresh jalapeno)
  • 1/2 cup(~26g) diced onion
  • 3 large cloves of garlic, minced
  • oil for frying
  • 8 large flour tortillas
  • 8 oz(~225g) shredded Monterey Jack cheese

-Sour cream sauce-

  • 2 Tbsp(~28g) butter
  • 2 Tbsp(~28g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup(~236ml) water
  • 1 cube chicken bouillon
  • 1/2 cup(~118ml) sourcream
  • salt and pepper to taste
