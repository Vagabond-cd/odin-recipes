Stir and Drop Sugar Cookies

Chrismas Cookies

Every year during the Christmas holidays my mother would make these sugar cookies. Helping her frost the cookies was one of the biggest highlights of the Christmas season and remains a beloved holiday tradition to this day. These cookies are great plain but are even better with frosting.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs (beaten)
  • 3/4 Cup Sugar
  • 2/3 Cup Oil(Vegtable, Canola, etc.)
  • 2 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Cups Flour
  • 2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Combine sugar, oil, vanilla and eggs into large mixing bowl.
  3. Sift baking powder, salt, and flour into oil mixture.
  4. Stir until combined
  5. Drop scoops of dough onto ungreased baking sheet 2 inches apart.
  6. Bake 8 to 10 minutes(till edges are golden brown).

-Vanilla Butter Frosting-

  • 1/4 Cup Softened Butter
  • 2 Cups Powdered Sugar
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1 Tbsp Milk
  • *Optional* Food Coloring

Combine all ingredient into mixing bowl and mix until smooth. Add food coloring if desired.
